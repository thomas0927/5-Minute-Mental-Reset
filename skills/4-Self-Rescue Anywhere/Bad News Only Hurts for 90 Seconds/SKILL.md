## Core Idea
Emotional chemistry lasts about 90 seconds. Freeze, label, shrink, act.

## When To Use
Sudden bad news or shock.

## Inputs
- Current situation and trigger
- Current anxiety intensity (0-10)

## Output
- A short, step-by-step action plan
- A reframe or label for the emotion

## Protocol
- Freeze for 90 seconds and do nothing.
- Label the emotion and score intensity 0-10.
- Run the 3P shrink questions: personalization, pervasiveness, permanence.
- Do one small action to break freeze.
- Prepare if-then plans for future bad news.

## Checks
- Does the user feel at least slightly more control after 2-5 minutes?
- Is the next action concrete and doable right now?
